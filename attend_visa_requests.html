Visa Requests

 

Visa Application

The requirements for the United States visa application may vary with country. For your convenience, if a letter of invitation is needed, we ask to provide the information listed below. For the detailed instructions, please refer to the United States Embassy/Consulate in your country/area.

 

Invitation letter

If you need an invitation letter, please feel free to contact our Registration Chair, _Registration Chair Name_. Please include the following information(s) in your email:

  • Registration number
  • Name
  • Job title/position
  • Date of birth
  • Passport ID
  • Country
  • Affiliation
  • Affiliation address
  • Post address
  • The paper title if you have an accepted paper
  • Names of the authors of your accepted paper

All identity information(s) should be consistent with your passport.
