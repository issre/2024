Visa Requests

 

If you need an invitation letter for a Japanese visa application, please contact the Registration Chair (issre2024reg@gmail.com) as early as possible. You need full payment of the registration fee before the visa request. Please indicate "ISSRE VISA REQUEST" as an email subject and include the following information:

  • Registration number
  • Name
  • Job title/position
  • Date of birth
  • Passport ID
  • Country
  • Affiliation
  • Affiliation address
  • Post address (Home)
  • Paper ID (if you have an accepted paper)
  • Paper title
  • Paper authors

All identity information should be consistent with your passport.
