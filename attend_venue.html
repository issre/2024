Conference Venue

 

Meyer Health Campus

 

The conference will take place at the Meyer Health Campus. The Meyer Health Campus is part of the Meyer Hospital which is a joint institution of the Tuscany Region Public Healthcare service and the University of Florence. It is one of the major and most qualified Italian Children Hospitals, both for healthcare service and for scientific research. The mission of the Meyer Health Campus is support personnel training and continuous education, largely leveraging simulation technologies and practices.

Florence is served by various bus and tram lines. The Meyer Health Campus is reachable within a 15-minute walk from the terminus of the T1 tram line. The address of the Meyer Health Campus:

Via Cosimo il Vecchio 26 | 50139 Firenze, Italy
