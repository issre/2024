Travel Information

 

For information on how to reach the venue, please visit the dedicated page.

Florence Airport (FLR) can be easily reached by plane from all the major airline hubs in Europe. Flights are available, for example, from Paris (Air France), London (British Airways), Amsterdam (KLM), Munich and Frankfurt (Lufthansa), Zurich and Geneva (Swiss), Vienna (Austrian Airlines), Rome (Alitalia), with convenient connections to all destinations worldwide. Direct flights from many cities in Europe are also available with Air Berlin, Brussels Airlines, Darwin Airline, Volotea, Vueling. Once at Florence Airport, you can easily reach the city center by taking the T2 tram line, which takes about 20 minutes.

Furthermore, both the Pisa and Bologna airports provide a viable alternative with additional flights and connections. In particular, from the Pisa airport (PSA) the city of Florence is easily reachable both by train (~1h travel) and bus (~1h travel). From the Bologna airport (BLQ) the city of Florence can be reached either by high-speed train in about an hour, or by bus in approximately an hour and a half.

Florence is served by various bus and tram lines that can be used for convenient city transportation. In particular, the venure of the conference is reachable within a 15-minute walk from the terminus of the T1 tram line.
