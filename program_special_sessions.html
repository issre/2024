Important dates

  • Author registration deadline: September 20th, 2024 (JST)
  • Early-bird registration deadline: September 27th, 2024 (JST)
  • Regular registration deadline: October 31st, 2024 (JST)
  • Invoice/receipt download deadline: November 30th, 2024 (JST)

Registration

  • The full conference registration gives access to all events in the four-day program, including receptions and banquet.
  • The workshop one-day registration gives access to only workshops, including workshop reception.
  • Extra tickets for receptions and banquet can be added.
  • All dates are set in Japan Standard Time (JST, UTC+9).
  • The maintenance of the registration site (confit) will take place on the following dates: August 8th, September 12th, October 10th, and November 14th.
  • Please email the Registration Chair (issre2024reg@gmail.com) if you need any registration assistance.

Author registration

  • At least one author for each accepted paper needs to complete an author full conference registration and payment by September 20th, 2024.
  • An author of a workshop paper can also choose an author workshop one-day registration if s/he attends only workshops.
  • The author registration can have an IEEE/CCF discount but not a student discount.
  • The same registration cannot cover multiple papers.

Non-author registration

  • Discounted early-bird registration rates will be offered until September 27th, 2024. Payment is required by this day.
  • Rate discounting is offered for students.
  • Co-authors of the papers already covered by author registrations are allowed non-author registrations.

Cancellation Policy

  • All refund/cancellation requests must be received in writing to issre2024reg@gmail.com.
  • There will be an administrative fee of 10% deducted from each refund before September 30th, 2024.
  • We will refund 50% of the registration payment amount if you cancel before October 25th, 2024.
  • There will be no refund for registrations canceled after October 25th, 2024.
