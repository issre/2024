Sponsorship Opportunities

 

Attracting international participants from academia as well as industry, IEEE ISSRE 2024 in Japan provides a great opportunity for corporate activities. Below are some examples of the benefits of different corporate support levels and packages. If you are interested in supporting IEEE ISSRE 2024 by a sponsorship or have any questions or suggestions, please do not hesitate to contact Masateru Tsunoda (tsunoda@info.kindai.ac.jp).

Platinum:

  • 1,000,000 JPY
  • Benefits: a, b, c, d

Gold:

  • 200,000 JPY
  • Benefits: b, c, d

Silver:

  • 100,000 JPY
  • Benefits: c, d

Bronze:

  • 50,000 JPY
  • Benefits: d

a. Sponsors can hold a special session organized by themselves.

b. One person joins ISSRE2024 for free.

c. Sponsors can exhibit their posters on the conference site.

d. Sponsors' logo are shown on the web site of ISSRE2024.
